Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cardestiny ("we", "us", the "App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App. By using Cardestiny you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account identifiers. When you sign in with Apple, we receive an Apple-provided user identifier, and — if you choose to share them — your name and email. With anonymous sign-in we create only a random identifier and collect no name or email.
  • Profile information you provide. A username, a chosen built-in avatar (no photo upload), and optional social handles you add.
  • Quiz results. Your answers produce a personality vector and type, stored so you can revisit readings and so your profile can be matched and displayed.
  • Purchase records. If you buy, our payment provider records the transaction and entitlement. We never receive or store your card number — payment is handled entirely by Apple.
  • On-device data. Your reading history and preferences are stored locally on your device.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell your data.

2. How we use information

  • To provide the core service: compute your personality, match your destiny card, save your readings.
  • To display your public profile (see §3) to other users.
  • To process and restore in-app purchases.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App.

3. Public profiles

If your profile is public, your username, avatar, personality type, and any social handles you added become visible to other users in the App's community features. Profiles are public by default; you can switch to private anytime on the Profile screen, after which only you can see your profile.

4. Service providers

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and App Store in-app purchases.
  • Supabase — secure cloud database and image storage hosting your profile and readings.
  • RevenueCat — validates and manages in-app purchase entitlements.

These providers process data only to provide their service to us.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your profile and readings until you delete them or request account deletion. You can make your profile private anytime, and request deletion of your account and data by emailing us. We delete data within 30 days of a verified request, except where retention is required by law.

6. Children

Cardestiny is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

7. International users

Your data may be processed on servers outside your country, including where our providers operate. If you are in the EEA or UK, our legal basis is performance of our service to you and your consent; you may access, correct, or delete your data and complain to your local authority.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes update the "Last updated" date above.
